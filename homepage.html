About Me

Hi! My name's Ben and I am a full-stack web-developer with additional experience in iOS programming. I am currently a junior at Boston College studying Computer Science and Information Systems. Coding became a part of my life in high school and I've never looked back since. The mastering of coding languages has an empowering effect and makes me feel like I can create anything I imagine. Because of this, I am always eager to constantly expanding my skillset!

Besides coding, some of my interests include basketball, gaming, writing film analysis, and adventuring outside.
